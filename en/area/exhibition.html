Museum/Science and Technology Museum and Exhibition facilities

    Our team of Museum and Themed Development is composed of an experienced professional team of world class. Our service is started from the project Feasibility Study; we analyse your project and implement a comprehensive assessment from the various aspects such as theme, social impact, visitors, venue specifications, cost, financing, construction, operation, maintenance and the like. We will ensure that your project is on the truck of success from the beginning.

    The concept and design for the themed venue are developed by taking the story line in the centre, in which the theme is interpreted by selecting a range of appropriate and suitable display technologies. Our design method emphasizes on the experience of the audiences, so that the design is not limited to the visual effects appeared; at the same time, the design applies the advanced display technologies combined with the planning of the relevant themed events, and therefore the audiences can understand the theme comprehensively from a self-involvement experience.

    Our services include feasibility study, theme planning, project management, concept development, construction, display concept and production, venue operation, and optimization of existing venue experience.
