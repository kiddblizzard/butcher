Construction Design

Shanghai Expo 2010 - Shanxi Pavilion

  • In China, a large number of iconic projects have emerged during the rapid globalization process. A lot of them were created, designed and engineered by the foreign designers and top design houses; normally they have extensive design experiences and excellent creation, innovation ability. Their participation has greatly influenced development process of modern design and engineering industry in China.

    During the participation process, the foreign designers, engineers, and their clients are facing many challenges when pursuing their design concept. An inappropriate decision can result in project delay, and over spending. Often the final look and feel is far from the original design intent, ended with failure. The two main challenges they facing in the process were

    1) the concept design and scheme design shall comply with local construction laws and regulations and industry design standard in China.

    2) The buildability of its Concept and Scheme Design under the local construction methods and technology in China.

    Nomura Construction has more than 16 years design and engineering experience in China; we witnessed the rapid development of regulating system in the construction industry, and we have built a mutual working relationship with the regulating bodies locally. Thus Nomura Construction can accurately control design schedule, and ensure that your capital programme goes smoothly during design stage.

    At construction design stage, Nomura Construction engineers know how to use our extensive experiences to challenge the local construction methods and technology under the condition of satisfying the regulations and design standards, to maximum faithful to the original design concept.

    Nomura Construction engineers have broad experiences cover all design aspects in the structure engineering, M&E, plumbing, civil engineering, and BMS. We are not only familiar with the regulations and design standards in China; we also have the knowledge of the design standards from the European countries, Asia countries, and North American; We consider these specifications during the construction design process.

    We are confident that Nomura Construction is capable of satisfying your Construction Design requirements, which have been proved by our outstanding performance to date.
