Themed Venue Development Consulting

Shanghai Expo 2010 - Cuba Pavilion

  • In 2005, Nomura Construction teamed-up an international consortium focused on the museum development consultancy market in China. Since then, Nomura Consortium has successfully delivered some of the best museums and pavilions across China, including the winning design of South Pacific Pavilions in 2010 Shanghai Expo.

    Nomura Consortium provides full consultancy services for the lifecycle of museum development, from Feasibility Study, Story Lines Development, Concept Development, Scheme Design, Interactive Technology, Construction Design, Project Management and Production.
