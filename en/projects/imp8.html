Liaoning science and technology museum

Project Description

Project name

Liaoning science and technology museum

Client

Liaoning science and technology museum

Project location

Liaoning, shenyang province

Project area

102,508㎡

Investment

1.5 billion RMB

Project duration

Dec, 2013

Award

won the “excellence award” of permanent exhibition overall design in the selection of Liaoning science and technology museum exhibition plan

Project Scope

concept design


Liaoning science and technology museum covers 102,508㎡， the planning area is 69100㎡, the main building have 3 levels above ground, total 31.9m high. Total investment is about 1.5 billion, will be the biggest science museum of northwest after completion.

Nomura join the museum concept design during civil works, Nomura’s design team made the concept design report to the Liaoning science association and organizing committee for several times, and won the excellence award. During the tender stage, Nomura participate the first, second, and third section of the concept design tender. In between, the concept tender include the concept design, signage design, exhibits design, exhibits technical explanation, and budget summary. The tender we supported which includes the complete set of plan, drawings, video received good reputation from Liaoning science association and organizing committee
