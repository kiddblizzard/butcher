Shanghai Expo 2010 - Vanke pavilion

Project Description

Project name

Shanghai Expo 2010 - Vanke pavilion

Client

Vanke Shanghai

Project location

Expo Park Shanghai 2010 Expo – Zone E

Project area

300㎡

Investment

RMB 600,000

Project duration

Aug, 2010-Oct, 2010

Award

Vanke Shanghai pavilion design won the silver award of”2010 China interior design week and the 8th China international interior design exhibition”

Project Scope

provide furniture and other items

PDF Download

PDF Download


The theme of Vanke pavilion is "the possibility of respect," the natural wheat straw was applied for building materials, is composed by seven independent of golden barrel-shaped building. At the outside, there’s more than 1000 m2 water around. The pavilion is also entitled as “2049”. The pavilion mainly tells the mutual respect of people, nature and city through 5 stories. The No.F building media broadcast hall originally, later revised as children VIP hall.

Nomura completed all the No.F building construction drawings. It used to be media broadcasting hall, was revised as children VIP hall after August. On considering Children was the main body, high glass environmental materials were applied to the carpet, paint and furniture. As the project are during the expo part open time, all the work should be done from the midnight until 6:00am.Most parts were completed in factory and installed on site. Thus it has very strict requirement for the drawings, as well as the site management. Nomura successfully finished the project, and was appointed as Vanke D&B strategy cooperative partners.
