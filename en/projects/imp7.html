Shanghai Pavilion on the 7th China Xinjiang Kashgar Central Asia& South Asia Trade Fair

Project Description

Project name

Shanghai Pavilion on the 7th China Xinjiang Kashgar Central Asia& South Asia Trade Fair

Client

Shanghai Domestic Cooperation & Exchange Center

Project location

Xinjiang Kashgar International Convention Centre

Project area

1518㎡

Investment

RMB 3,000,000

Project duration

March 2011-July 2011

Award

Shanghai Pavilion won the award of excellence for display poject

Project Scope

Conceptual design; Design development; Plant production; Construction and operation on site


The project was funded by Shanghai Municipal Government and attracted dozens of Shanghai outstanding enterprises to participate in. The Fair is held to strengthen economic and trade exchange between Shanghai and Central Asia, South Asia and Xinjiang regions and to promote economic development of these areas.

Nomura Construction completed concept design, design development, display project, multimedia project and part of operation management work. Nomura’s designers made a unified plan to the 1518 M2 Shanghai Pavilion. Under the premise of uniform style and clear regional division Nomura completed booth design of Shanghai enterprises, and completed display project of Shanghai Pavilion. Since Xinjiang Province is relatively underdeveloped and lags in technology, the supplier system of display project there is imperfect as well, many of the exhibits were customized by Nomura in Shanghai and then transported to the site for assembly. It’s more than 4000 kilometers from Shanghai to Xinjiang, any errors were unacceptable on the way. Nomura made detailed confirmation to the exhibition props of each booth before transportation to ensure the site installation work. Nomura’s fine and high-quality work was highly praised by the participants and the organizer. Shanghai Pavilion won the award of excellence for display project.
