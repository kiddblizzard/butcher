HAKKASAN Michelin Restaurant

Project Description

Project name

HAKKASAN Michelin Restaurant

Client

Hakkasan (Shanghai) Ltd

Project location

5F, Bund 18

Project area

1700㎡

Investment

30 million RMB

Project duration

Dec 2012-July 2011

Project Scope

Construction design, fit-out, M&E work, weak current work


The project is the Michelin restaurant in China, located in a famous historical building. Hakkasan restaurant is focusing on Chinese food. The concept design is finished by the famous French GBRH.

Nomura completed for the construction design, also was appointed as the main contractor. Except the fit-out work, M&E work, plumbing work, weak current work, Nomura responsible for the management work of furniture and kitchen work. Nomura team was built by the professional engineers from UK, HK, and Japan according to Hakkasan’s high standard. Most fit-out work are done in the factory, and installed on site. The management work for the subcontractors should be very careful as each detail shall be confirmed by many sides. Good faith has been built between Nomura and client during the work. The project is proceeding smoothly on the way.
