Muji exclusive shop Fit-out project

Project Description

Project name

Muji exclusive shop Fit-out project

Client

Muji (Shanghai) Co., Ltd

Project location

Shanghai, Beijing

Project area

1000㎡

Investment

RMB 400,000

Project duration

2009-2010

Award

Muji store got the Silver Award of “the 7th China International Interior Design Biennial” by the Chinese Interior Decoration Association.

Project Scope

Construction design; Fit-out; M&E project; Weak current project


MUJI is a world famous chain store of living supplies. Nomura Construction has been MUJI’s main design and fit-out service provider since the year of 2009.

Nomura completed construction design of all Muji stores constructed by us. Since Muji stores are throughout the country, Nomura’s project management team has the capability of design and construction in China’s different provinces and cities. Nomura’s project management team can complete relevant governmental procedures in China’s different provinces and cities. These capabilities well supported Muji’s development in China. Meanwhile Nomura established off-site construction management system, through which we can make integrated control and management to the project’s quality, cost and duration.
