Shanghai Expo 2010 - Cuba Pavilion

Project Description

Project name

Shanghai Expo 2010 - Cuba Pavilion

Client

Cuba consulate of Shanghai

Project location

Expo Park Shanghai 2010 Expo – Zone C

Project area

500㎡

Investment

USD 800,000

Project duration

Sep, 2009-April, 2010

Project Scope

Construction Design; Permitting, Fit-Out; Display Installation; Multi-Media Display Design and Installation Facility maintenance, Operation management

PDF Download

PDF Download


Cuba pavilion built a city center square scene, which was composed of by some architecture like post office, bar, and store and so on, especially the site cigar production and sales area. Each visitor can feel like entered the Havana street and realize the theme: provide the equal opportunity for citizens to let them actively to join the city construction and change.

Nomura completed all the construction drawings. The project is almost a stage engineering, on considering the designer’s high requirements, Nomura carefully researched and analyzed each architecture, took the reasonable process on considering the safety factors. Cuba is the famous cigar production country, the yellow building is a knock-off of old cigar production workshop, it perfectly reprints the cigar production scene. The difficulty of the building is to use wood for building. After research, Nomura had some fire-resistant treatment and finally reach the fire requirement.

During the light design, on considering the film effect and stage performance requirement, we edit the scattered film materials and integrate those to be a complete film. Use 3 projectors to realize a full image and project on 16*3m wall.

For the waves of the sea, the owner only put forward such a concept idea, did not provide any element to us, we completed the materials selection, design and production. The final work receives high praise from the ambassador of China and Cuba curator’s satisfaction.
