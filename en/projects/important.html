  • Shanghai Expo 2010 - Pacific Pavilion

    Pacific Pavilion

    The Pacific pavilion is the third largest Pavilion at Expo 2010 after China and the African Joint pavilion. The Pavilion includes 15 pacific island countries and 2 tourism organizations, is one of the most characteristic pavilion of 11 joint pavilions in Sshanghai Eexpo 2010.

  • Shanghai Expo 2010 - Peru Pavilion

    Peru Pavilion

    The Peru pavilion theme is “food factory, feeding the city”. In the center of pavilion, there’s a steel structure-flat-topped pyramid-symbol of Peru earliest buildings, also the symbol of early civilization of Peru. The visitors can walk up to the pyramid, and watch the movie about the development, ancient and modern civilization of Peru through a giant 360 °circle screen. The Peruvian restaurant greatly shows Peru food culture，and dynamically reflect the theme of the pavilion.

  • Shanghai Expo 2010 - Kyrgyz Pavilion

  • Shanghai Expo 2010 - Tajikistan Pavilion

    Tajikistan Pavilion

    The theme of Tajikistan Pavilion is “The city changes and the good life”. Nomura Construction duplicated the famous Tajik Castle design element to make the entrance door.   • Shanghai Expo 2010 - Brunei Pavilion

    Brunei Pavilion

    The Peru pavilion theme is “Now for the future”. The outside decoration and entrance running drum visually display Brunei natural and human impression. The shining blue glass floor runs through the whole pavilion, reflect the characteristics that the local residents mostly live in the wooden house upon the water. The pavilion has 5 galleries, tell the story of China and Brunei friendship, elegant royal supplies, Brunei development and petroleum industry, traditional culture and coffee. The 4D cinema let people feel the beautiful natural environment and the diversity of species.
