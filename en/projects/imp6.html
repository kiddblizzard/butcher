Ashley Booth at 2011 China International Furniture Expo

Project Description

Project name

Ashley Booth at 2011 China International Furniture Expo

Client

Wanvog Furniture (Kunshan) Co., Ltd.

Project location

Shanghai New International Expo Center

Project area

600㎡

Investment

RMB 1,330,000

Project duration

Sept. 2011

Project Scope

Construction design; Booth manufacture; Facility maintenance; Booth dismantle


The project is a steel structure booth of two layers.

Nomura Construction won the bidding of Ashley booth display project at 2011 International Furniture Expo. Our exhibition department discussed a lot with Ashley American concept designer through long-distance communication and completed design development work. The booth is a steel structure of two layers. This is a very complicated structure design, and during the exhibition period there will be a large audience on the 2nd floor, so it has very high request for safety. Nomura has only two days on the site to build the booth including the installation of steel structure. To avoid any problem, Nomura exercised the construction in the plant in advance to ensure the construction quality and time to meet client requirements. Through Nomura’s efforts, the project was completed successfully and we got high praise from Ashley- our client and the designer.
