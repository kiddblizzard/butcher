Shanghai Expo 2010 – Armenia Pavilion

Project Description

Project name

Shanghai Expo 2010 – Armenia Pavilion

Client

Armenia Development Agency

Project location

Expo Park Shanghai 2010 Expo – Zone C

Project area

350㎡

Investment

USD 350,000

Project duration

November 2009 - April 2010

Project Scope

Construction Design; Permitting, Fit-Out; Display Installation; Model making; Multi-Media Display Design and Installation; Facility Maintenance.

PDF Download


The theme of Armenia Pavilion is “The city of the world” The project is made up of the following parts:
The 7-meter high arcuate display wall on the periphery of the pavilion; The 12 relief wall of 4.6 meters high on both sides of the pavilion entrance; The 4.6-meter high and 10-meter long arcuate projection wall; On the model platform in the center of the pavilion are showed architectural models of the cities in the world; In the pavilion there is also a gift shop, an office and a warehouse.

Nomura Construction completed all construction design work. The design of 7-meter high arcuate display wall on the periphery of the pavilion requires light steel as the structure; besides, it is an individual wall having no connection with the original building. Nomura’s designers communicated a lot with the Architectural Design Institute and made accurate calculation through professional software to check safety and stability requirements of the wall structure. Based on this, we made the following adjustment to the design: for the internal structure of the wall, we used steel structure combined with auxiliary light steel, through which the whole arc wall is connected as a whole. The rectangular steel pipe of the longitudinal wall are connected and fixed to the ground by chemical bolts and plates.

Black grille is used as suspended ceiling of the pavilion with an area of 322 square meters. While for Such a large area of suspended ceiling, the original building has only 4 supporting points for fixing. Through professional calculation, we used well-shaped steel structure as the fixed base of the grid, which is equivalent to increase the hanging points of the grid. It effectively ensured security, stability, smoothness and bearing strength of the grid ceiling.

In addition, the relief wall, projection wall and arcuate display wall is connected both in straight pull and cable-stayed connection mode. In that way the three walls are connected into a whole, which ensured security and stability of the whole wall of the pavilion.

Nomura Construction carefully selected model making company to complete the dozens of models designed by world famous architectural designers. In the gap between the models we used LED screen to present detailed introduction and overall stereo effect. Through circular layout of the pavilion with reasonable layout of the sound, light, electricity and exhibits, we perfectly achieved the effect that the designers would like to show.
