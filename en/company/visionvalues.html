Vision & values

  • Our Visions and values

    Create a better quality of life


    Visions

    Continual improvement and become the best design, construction and management company in the build industry of China


    • Become the trusted partner of our customers / suppliers, and work cooperatively to pursue the success together. Keep our promises, grow and improve the business at the back of our customers and suppliers success.

    • Our employees are proud to be a member of Nomura Construction, and our suppliers feel honoured to become our partners. We create opportunities for our employees and suppliers, and their success is our success.

    • Be responsible to the society, and focus on the management of health, safety and environment. Actively promote and participate in the development of industrial standards, and become the leader of industrial development and the model of social responsibility.


    Our values

    The Nomura Construction corporate values are gradually formed in our15 years operation development; and they are the cornerstone of our success and guiding our sustainable development to date. The point of adhering to our held values is the guarantee of ensuring the permanent development of the company.



    Health & Safety

    Zero Accident is our unwavering goal. Creating a safe and healthy environment for our employees, suppliers and our customers is the responsibility and obligation of Nomura Construction


    Integrity

    Fairness, honesty and integrity are our principles, and the uncompromising of these principles are the heart of our company.


    Mutual Respect

    Mutual respect, openness, team work and trust are the principles for the cooperation of our company to our employees, suppliers and customers.


    Pursuit of Excellence

    Never stop and feel content, continually innovate, apply advanced technologies, and take challenges of limitations are the key concepts for us pursuing excellence. That took us to the leading position of our industry in China.


    Sustainable Development

    Draw the development plan based on the sustainable principles, act at the social responsibilities, and being responsible for the long-term interest of our employees, suppliers, customers and the world.
