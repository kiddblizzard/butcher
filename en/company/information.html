Nomura Construction Introduction

  • Nomura Construction

    Nomura Construction is a world-class local construction services business operating across the construction lifecycle, with leading position in major markets in PR China.

    Over 15 years organic growth in China, focused on the private sector, Nomura Construction is becoming a rising star for the industry bring in cutting-edge concept designs, technologies and engineering to the China’s ever dynamic assets development markets. We provide the one-stop solutions to our clients / partners from Feasibility Study, Concept Development, Buildability Analysis, Scheme Design, Construction Design, Permitting, Construction and Assets Operation.

    Nomura Construction takes your business needs in the centre core of our approach; we value our contribution not only by success delivering your projects but enhance your business advantages in the markets. Our approach was enabled by our in-house bespoke advanced Programme / Project Management methodology, which tested and approved by our success to date in China.
