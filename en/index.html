SAFETY FIRST

Safety is a value that is fundamental to our culture. Nomura Construction has earned an industry-leading record of achieving zero lost-time incidents on projects national wide. Year after year, our zero accidents philosophy helps put our safety performance right at the top of the industry.

Our Goal: Zero Accidents

  Days  


At Nomura Construction, we believe that every accident, and therefore every injury, is preventable, and we embed that philosophy into every Nomura project through a combination of technical field procedures and ongoing training programs. Every employee has stop-work authority—if it's not safe, don't do it. We also ask our subcontractors and partners to adopt our commitment to safety and health. The result is exceptional safety performance, even in hazardous work environments, severe weather, and remote locations.



NOMURA CONSTRUCTION

Nomura Construction is a world-class local construction services business operating across the construction lifecycle, with leading position in major markets in PR China. Over 15 years organic growth in China, focused on the private sector, Nomura Construction is becoming a rising star for the industry bring in cutting-edge concept designs, technologies and engineering to the China’s ever dynamic assets development markets.
