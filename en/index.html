About Us


Quality and Hygiene are two things we hold sacred.

The first Butcher Shop was established in 2004 in Seaworld, Shekou to cater for the needs of the local expat community who longed for the ability to buy fresh meat and cold cuts.

We have established buying arrangements so that all our Beef is imported following strict quality assurance guidelines and hormone free. Also to alleviate any concerns regarding local Chicken products due to Bird Flu we import whole chickens directly from Argentina.

With central production and distribution all opertations are overseen by the manager, who has over 15 years working in the industry to bring you clean, quality products.
